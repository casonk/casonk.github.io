`wiring-harness` centralizes private-service ingress concerns such as Caddy configuration, mTLS certificate generation, DNS records, and mobileconfig export for trusted devices.

It lets sibling repositories register private sites once and reuse a shared provisioning path instead of duplicating service-specific ingress setup.
