`shock-relay` provides a unified messaging surface for automation scripts, agents, and infrastructure. The current implementation exposes provider-specific entry points across Signal, Telegram, WhatsApp, Twilio SMS, and Gmail IMAP.

It is the shared portfolio home for outbound notification and messaging workflows.
