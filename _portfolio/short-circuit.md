`short-circuit` is a WireGuard setup and configuration utility for private remote access. It maintains profile-based templates for host-only and wider home-LAN access patterns while keeping real keys and endpoints out of git.

The repo focuses on reproducible VPN setup for authenticated SMB, HTTPS, and SSH access through a private tunnel.
